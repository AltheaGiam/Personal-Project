About Gudetama

Gudetama is a character created by Sanrio He is depicted as a lazy egg who cannot be bothered to do anything with his day. He prefers to lay about and sleep, wrapped up in his bacon blanket. In Japan, Gudetama is is often used for theme pop-up cafes serving egg-related dishes.
